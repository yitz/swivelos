Our Model

The Circular Economy in Action

Instead of sending perfectly functional furniture to the dump, we intercept it, restore it, and give it a second life. It's a simple model with an extraordinary impact — for the environment and your bottom line.

How It Works

1
Corporate Surplus Identified
Companies downsize, relocate, or upgrade — creating surplus premium furniture that would otherwise end up in landfills.
2
Swivel Acquires & Transports
Our logistics team handles complete decommissioning — evaluation, disassembly, and transport to our NY metro warehouse.
3
Professional Refurbishment
Every piece is inspected, cleaned, repaired, and restored to like-new condition by our experienced refurbishment team.
4
You Furnish & Save
Your business gets premium furniture at 50-80% off retail — and the planet gets a break from unnecessary manufacturing and waste.
5
The Cycle Continues
When you're ready to upgrade, we can buy it back and refurbish it again — keeping the circular economy spinning.
